MT. PALAOPAO WATER SOURCE 2

As you approach a hidden cave along the trail, a serene and refreshing water source comes into view just outside its entrance. The spring-fed water trickles down the rocky base of the cave, flowing gently over smooth stones and pooling into a clear, bubbling stream. The water then winds its way through half-bamboo canes, their slender stalks arching gracefully over the water, creating a natural bridge of green.
