WELCOME TO VANGUARD

Discover the beauty of Mt. Palaopao a hidden gem offering scenic trails, pristine water sources, and an ideal campsite for nature enthusiasts. This website provides all the information you need to plan your trip, including trail maps, water source locations, camping tips, and safety guidelines. Get ready to experience the great outdoors at Mt. Palaopao!
