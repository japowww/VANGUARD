Fun Fact about Mt. Palaopao

As we made our way back from our wild journey through the untouched wonders of nature, we stumbled upon a curious sight on the trail near Mount Palaopao—a tarantula! Despite their fearsome reputation, tarantulas are actually quite shy creatures and prefer to avoid human contact. This particular one, with its thick, hairy legs and slow, deliberate movements, was likely on the hunt for insects to feast on.
